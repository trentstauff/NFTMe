What is NFTMe?

NFTMe is the result of a fun coding challenge posed by Shopify.


Originally, I set out to create a regular image repository- but I knew that I had to put my
own fintech twist on things while also creating a shop-like marketplace (just like one that could be made from using Shopify).
So, I decided to tailor the platform towards NFTs (non-fungible tokens)!

NFTs are a type of data stored on the blockchain, meaning that each NFT is unique.

There is a huge buzz out there surrounding NFTs so I took the opportunity of the presented challenge to hop on the trend!

I hope you enjoy playing around with this fullstack application! -Trent
